I'm Muhammad Jaseem — I work on the systems and software that keep computers, offices, and small businesses running smoothly.

My work covers the full range of practical IT: installing and configuring operating systems, setting up and licensing PC software, troubleshooting hardware and performance issues, and building the digital tools organizations actually use day to day — from data systems to custom web-based applications.
